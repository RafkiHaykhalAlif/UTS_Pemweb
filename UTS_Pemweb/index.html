About Anthony Sinisuka Ginting

Anthony Sinisuka Ginting is an Indonesian badminton player who specializes in men's singles. He has been a member of the Indonesian national badminton team since 2014 and has represented Indonesia in various international tournaments. Ginting started his professional career in the world of badminton and quickly demonstrated his talent at the international level. He is known for his aggressive style of play and high speed, which has made him one of the top players in the world. As one of the leading players in Indonesia, Ginting is an inspiration for many young players and badminton fans in the country.
